Implementation Org · Board Update

AI as an Enabler of Scalable Implementations

From system configurators to activation consultants — embedding AI to increase scalability, improve quality, and reduce manual work, while shifting team capacity toward customer outcomes.
Objective: Deliver greater business impact, not just system delivery. We are freeing the team from repetitive work (manual reports, coordination, documentation) so capacity flows into analysis, brainstorming, problem-solving, and proactive account activation. The transformation positions Implementation as a measurable lever on customer outcomes.
